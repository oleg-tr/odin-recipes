Pasta alla puttanesca

Ingredients

  • pasta
  • tomato passata
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Olive oil
  • Capers and olives

Steps

  1. Salt boiling water and put pasta inside. Wait for 7 minutes until it is al dente.
  2. Prepare tomato sauce on a frying pan. Olive oil, passata, capers and olives.
  3. Add two table spoons of pasta water, stir.
  4. Add pasta.
