Pirogi

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Butter
  • Olive oil
  • Rosemary

Steps

  1. Season well all sides of your steak with salt, pepper and garlic powder.
  2. Heat up your cast iron and add a good amount of olive oil. Gently put the steak there for two minutes.
  3. Turn around your steak and let it roast for one minute. Add butter and pour some of it on the steak when it melts.
  4. Add rosemary and leave it all for another minute.
  5. Let the meat rest for five minutes at room temperature.
  6. Cut it to thin slices and enjoy your meal.
